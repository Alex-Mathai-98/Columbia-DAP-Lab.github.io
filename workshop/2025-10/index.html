AI Agents for Work

Columbia University | October 2025 (Date TBD)

How can AI Agents
streamline work and operations?

This 2-day workshop brings together industry leaders with academic experts at Columbia University to share the latest innovations and challenges for how AI agents can streamline work and operations.

The first day will be a series of public keynotes, talks and panel discussions from industry and academic experts. The second day will be a private workshop for partners affiliated with the Data, Agents and Processes Lab (DAPLab).
